Key Sustainability Milestones

1994

Environmental Leadership Program

Intel launched comprehensive environmental initiatives, establishing the foundation for decades of sustainability leadership. This included waste reduction programs, energy efficiency measures, and the beginning of our commitment to environmental stewardship in the semiconductor industry.

2008

Renewable Energy Leadership

Becomes the largest corporate purchaser of renewable energy...

Renewable Energy Leadership

Intel became the largest voluntary corporate purchaser of renewable energy in the United States, purchasing over 2.5 billion kWh annually. This milestone demonstrated our commitment to clean energy and significantly reduced our carbon footprint across global operations.

2012

Water Conservation Breakthrough

Achieves net positive water usage through innovative conservation...

Water Conservation Breakthrough

Intel achieved net positive water usage, meaning we restored more water to local communities than we consumed in our operations. Through advanced water recycling technologies and conservation efforts, we set new industry standards for responsible water stewardship.

2020

Carbon Neutral Goal

Commits to net-zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2040...

Carbon Neutral Goal

Intel announced our most ambitious sustainability goal yet: achieving net-zero greenhouse gas emissions across global operations by 2040. This commitment includes investments in renewable energy, energy efficiency improvements, and innovative technologies to reduce our environmental impact.
